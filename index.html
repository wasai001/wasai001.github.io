The Application of CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) in Architecture Design

July 21, 2019
Computational Simulation 电脑模拟
Karamay is on the most north west side of China. The city is famous for its extreme cold and windy winter. To design a comfort external space for local resident to work or exercise outside during the winter, we leverage the technology of CFD (computational fluid dynamics) to simulate air movement in the district. With the simulation result, we can modify the building and courtyard’s shape to intervene the air flow to avoid wind corridor and air static area.
